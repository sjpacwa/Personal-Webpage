General Goals

There were a wide variety of general goals that I set out to acheive with this new CSS style. One of them was to make the appearance of my website not only pleasing on the eyes, but something that could be recognized easily. Furthermore, I wanted something that I could modify easily while still keeping the general feel and look. Because of this there were a large variety of choices that were made in order to create a form of standardization. Something that I wanted to get across in the design of my webpage was a certain level of minimalism. Because of this the use of serif fonts is limited and the page itself offers no serious distractions. This allows a high level of focus to be given to the message of the text itself. Once downside of a minimalistic deisgn is that it may appear simple to create and does not garner the respect that some websites have such as Nvidia and instead causes it to have more in common with My Summer Car.
